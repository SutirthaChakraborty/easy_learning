What We Offer

Built for Every Learner

Every feature is designed with accessibility, engagement, and joy at its core.

🎮

Gamified Lessons

Earn XP, unlock badges, and level up as you complete lessons. Learning feels like an adventure.

📖

Dyslexia Mode

Switch to OpenDyslexic font, wider letter spacing, and warm cream backgrounds to reduce visual stress.

🔊

Click-to-Read

Tap any word or paragraph and hear it read aloud, clearly and at the perfect pace.

🧩

Mini-Games

Word puzzles, memory matching, and pattern games that reinforce concepts through play.

📊

Progress Reports

Parents and teachers get clear, friendly dashboards showing each child's growth over time.

🤖

AI Personalisation

Adaptive difficulty powered by AI ensures lessons are always just the right challenge.
